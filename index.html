The signal

Four states. One clear answer.

Every day Spending Pulse calculates your safe daily spend — your remaining balance minus your safety floor, divided over the next 30 days, minus upcoming bills. Then it tells you exactly where you stand.

No percentages to interpret. No category budgets to chase. Just a number and a status.

Safe

You have room to spend. Your balance is on track and above your safety floor.

Close to limit

You're using most of today's allowance. Spend carefully for the rest of the day.

At the limit

Today's safe-to-spend has been fully used. Any extra comes from tomorrow's allowance.

Overspent

You've gone past today's limit. Your projected balance is below your safety floor.
